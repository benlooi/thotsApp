In Woodgrove Secondary School, one of the four desired traits of a Woodgrover is to be a “Compassion leader with a passion to serve”. To help students achieve this desired trait, the school has developed its own distinctive programme known as Community Youth Leadership (CYL) Programme. The school CYL aims to nurture students to achieve the 3A student outcomes. (Awareness, Affection, Ability to Act).

Awareness

Students having the awareness of the various needs of the different community sectors in Singapore and beyond.

Affection

Students having compassion for others, feeling committed and motivated to meet the community needs.

Ability to Act

Students having the confidence and skills to meet the community needs.

The CYL progamme design is anchored on the 3A4P model, and in this assessment, you will be conducting a self-assessment to monitor your own development as a community youth leader based on this model. We hope you will answer the questions truthfully and it should take approximately 15 minutes to complete.

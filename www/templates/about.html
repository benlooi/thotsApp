This is a mobile app developed by Woodgrove Secondary School, Singapore. The app has two components: Reflection and Assessment. The reflection component aims to nurture students to be reflective individuals using the DEAL model (Ash & Clayton, 2009). The app is able to capture students’ learning experiences and their reflective thoughts throughout their entire secondary school education, especially their service learning experiences. The assessment component is a developmental tool developed according to Woodgrove’s 3A4P Community Youth Leadership (CYL) student competencies indictors. The self-assessment tool allows youths to be aware of their strengths and area for improvements, and nurture them to be community youth leaders (CYL).

This mobile app is powered by Singapore National Youth Council (NYC)
